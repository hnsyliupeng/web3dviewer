web3dviewer

Demonstration of web3dviewer component.

  • Viewer 1 - Demonstration uses CanvasRenderer and presents Walt Disney's head / Cube mesh
  • Viewer 2 - Demonstration uses CanvasRenderer and presents progressive mesh (leg model)
  • Viewer 3 - Demonstration chooses renderer (WebGL if possible) and presents Walt Disney's head / Cube mesh
  • Viewer 3 - Two independent web3dviewer components in single window.
